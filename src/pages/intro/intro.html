Spiela is a collaborative network empowering young people as a whole, but with a particular mission to engage those from challenging backgrounds and ensure that they have a safe and supportive space in which to engage and network with others.

Collaborating

To begin collaborating just click on Start Collaboration. Give your collaboration a name and give your collaboration keywords to help other users find it. Click on Publish, and you will be redirected to a page where you can write your collaboration and add an image.

Inside a Post

The top of the post is the authors canvas. Read and then engage with whatever is written on the canvas.

Add comment field

To connect with the author or anybody else within the post, just use the @ feature and type in the name of the user you want to respond to. This can also be used to invite other members into the post.

Explore page

Here you can find every spiela members collaborative posts.

Hot topic

The most popular/commented collaborations appear here. Any post with 25 comments turns amber yellow and posts with 50+ comments turns red.

Bulletin board

This is where you are notified when you are tagged in a post.

Influencers

Here you can find influential spiela members in different industries who you can begin talking to and collaborate with.

Partners

This is where you can find out which organisations /recruiters we have partnered with and the opportunities they are advertising.

The main goal is to create a tool that provides opportunities through collaboration whilst simultaneously providing more information on various topics. We believe everyone is influential in some way so anyone is allowed to join, contribute and share their view on the platform for the world to see.
